What is this page?

This tool is designed for musicians, learners, and enthusiasts to analyze any music or sound they want to understand. Simply drag and drop your music file into the left-hand area, and we’ll break it down into its musical elements such as chords, rhythm, notes, and structure. A fun, easy way to learn music!
